Planet Overview

Kepler-452b is an exoplanet orbiting the sun-like star Kepler-452. It was discovered by NASA's Kepler Space Telescope and announced in 2015. Often called "Earth's cousin," it's the first near-Earth-size planet found in the habitable zone of a star similar to our Sun.
