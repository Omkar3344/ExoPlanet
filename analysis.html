Key Insights

Model Strengths

  • High precision (91.7%) minimizes false positives
  • Excellent AUC-ROC (94.3%) shows strong discriminative ability
  • Consistent performance across different planet types
  • Robust to noisy or incomplete data

Areas for Improvement

Future Directions

  • Collaboration with international space agencies
